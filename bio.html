Hey there, my name is Francis Oboi, and I'm a web developer and graphic designer with a passion for social work. I am 25 years old and currently reside in Kampala, Uganda. Growing up, I was always drawn to technology and design. I loved tinkering with gadgets and creating art. When I went to University, I decided to pursue my passions and earned a degree in Telecommunications Engineering. In the due course of my engineering degree, I also did a number of certificate courses in web design, graphic design, cyber security.

I landed my first job as a web developer and graphic designer in December 2020 while I was in my first year at University, and I loved every minute of it. I was able to use my creativity and technical skills to create stunning websites and visual designs that helped my clients achieve their online goals. But I knew I wanted to do more than just create beautiful things, I wanted to use my skills to make a positive impact on the world.

That's when I started volunteering with non-profit organizations and using my web development and graphic design skills to help them spread their message and achieve their mission. It was incredibly rewarding to know that my work was making a difference in people's lives.

As I continued to volunteer, I realized that my passion for social work was just as strong as my passion for technology and design. That's why I've made it a mission to use my skills to make a positive impact on the world. Whether I'm creating a website or designing a poster, I want to showcase my creativity, I want to bring ideas into life and most of all, I want to be part of the journey of building a brand

So contact me whenever you are ready. Lets work together and create magic.
